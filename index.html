Pyramid: ARIMA estimators for Python¶

Pyramid brings R’s beloved auto.arima to Python, making an even stronger case for why you don’t need R for data science. Pyramid is 100% Python + Cython and does not leverage any R code, and implements a single, easy-to-use scikit-learn-esque estimator.
